About Me

Hi, I'm Marco Gancitano. I'm a data engineer living in Hoboken, New Jersey.


I am currently Data Engineer at Bloomberg L.P. where I get to combine my love for programming, statistics, and data analytics. At Bloomberg, I work primarily in Python to develop quality control and quality assurance data pipelines for our products.


I started at Bloomberg after graduating from Stevens Institute of Technology in 2019 with a B.S. in Quantitative Finance. While at Stevens, I interned as a Trade Assistant at INTL FCStone and a Quantitative Analyst at Marto Capital.
